🌌 Allah Shayari

Poetry glorifying Allah’s mercy, beauty and oneness

\"Jab bhi gham mile, dil ne Allah ko pukara,
Har mod par uski rehmat ka sahara mila.\"

\"Na ho himmat se kaam le, uski rehmat hai kaafi,
Jisne samandar paal diye, woh teri zindagi sambhalega.\"

\"Na roshni ki zarurat, na zuban ki,
Allah sunta hai dil ki bhi baatein.\"
